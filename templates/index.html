NUMBER OF REPORTED MALARIA CASES BETWEEN 2009 AND 2019

The graphs below show the number of reported malaria cases in all African countries from the year 2019 to 2019. The purpose of this visualization is to show the trend in malaria cases over a range of ten years in order to control and reduce the amount of cases. Malaria is a disease caused by a parasite. The parasite is spread to humans through the bites of infected mosquitoes. People who have malaria usually feel very sick with a high fever and shaking chills.
